About GrainPalette

A Deep Learning Odyssey in Rice Type Classification

ðŸŽ¯ Project Overview

GrainPalette is a deep learning-based web application that classifies images of rice grains into one of five types: Arborio, Basmati, Ipsala, Jasmine, and Karacadag. It uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) with transfer learning from MobileNetV2 to perform accurate rice type predictions.

ðŸ”¬ Technology Stack

Deep Learning: TensorFlow, Keras
Model: CNN with MobileNetV2 Transfer Learning
Backend: Flask, Python
Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Image Processing: PIL, NumPy
Data: Kaggle Rice Image Dataset

ðŸŒ¾ Rice Types

Arborio

Short-grain rice from Italy, perfect for risotto with its creamy texture.

Basmati

Long-grain aromatic rice from India and Pakistan, known for its fragrance.

Ipsala

Turkish rice variety with medium grains, commonly used in Turkish cuisine.

Jasmine

Fragrant long-grain rice from Thailand, slightly sticky when cooked.

Karacadag

Turkish rice variety grown in the Karacadag region, known for quality.
